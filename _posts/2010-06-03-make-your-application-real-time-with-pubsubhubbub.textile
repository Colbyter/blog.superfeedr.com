-Earlier in May- Last month, I was given the opportunity by my friend "Brett":http://www.onebigfluke.com/ to "present PubSubHubbub at Google IO":http://code.google.com/events/io/2010/sessions/real-time-apps-pubsubhubbub.html on stage with him. It was a great experience, and if you want to learn a bit more about PubSubHubbub and Superfeedr, I'd suggest you take the time to look at it!

bq. _This session will go over how to add support for the PubSubHubbub protocol to your website. You'll learn how to turn Atom and RSS feeds into real-time streams. We'll go over how to consume real-time data streams and how to make your website reactive to what's happening on the web right now._
